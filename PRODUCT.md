# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Affluent clients seeking bespoke floral arrangements for weddings, corporate events, private gatherings, and luxury gifting. They value artistry over commodity, understand the difference between a bouquet and a statement piece, and are willing to invest in craft.

## Product Purpose

Golden Bouquet is a premium floral design studio that creates sculptural, intentional arrangements. The site communicates artistic vision, builds desire, and drives consultation bookings. Success is measured in qualified inquiries from clients who understand what they are buying.

## Brand Personality

Refined. Curatorial. Confident. The studio operates like a private gallery, not a flower shop. Each arrangement is treated as an art object, not a commodity. The voice is knowledgeable without being precious.

## Anti-references

Generic floral e-commerce templates. Pink-and-cream wedding palettes. Cluttered "bouquet builder" interfaces. Clip-art botanical illustrations. "Fresh flowers delivered" messaging. Instagram-aesthetic flat lays with scattered petals.

## Design Principles

1. **Treat each arrangement as sculpture.** Photography and layout should feel gallery-like, not catalog.
2. **Let whitespace speak.** Abundance of negative space communicates premium positioning.
3. **Lead with artistry, not logistics.** Portfolio speaks first; service details second.
4. **Curated, not comprehensive.** Show selected work, not everything ever made.
5. **Build desire, then convert.** Every section should deepen the client's imagination before asking for contact.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Full keyboard navigation. Reduced motion support. Images with descriptive alt text.
